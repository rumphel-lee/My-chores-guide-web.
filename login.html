How to Do Laundry

  1. Sort clothes by color (lights, darks, whites).
  2. Check labels for washing instructions.
  3. Load the washing machine (don‚Äôt overfill).
  4. Add detergent (and fabric softener if needed).
  5. Select wash settings (temperature, cycle).
  6. Start the machine and wait until it finishes.
  7. Transfer clothes to the dryer (or hang to dry).
  8. Fold and store clean clothes.

How to Clean the House

  1. Declutter ‚Äì put away items lying around.
  2. Dust surfaces (tables, shelves, furniture).
  3. Sweep or vacuum floors.
  4. Mop floors (for tiled/wooden areas).
  5. Clean windows & mirrors with glass cleaner.
  6. Wipe down kitchen & bathroom counters.
  7. Empty trash bins and replace liners.
  8. Final check ‚Äì make sure each room looks tidy.

How to Wash the Dishes

  1. Scrape leftovers into the trash/compost.
  2. Rinse dishes with water.
  3. Fill sink/basin with warm, soapy water.
  4. Wash dishes (start with glasses, then plates, then pots/pans).
  5. Scrub tough stains with a sponge or brush.
  6. Rinse with clean water.
  7. Place on drying rack or wipe dry with a towel.
  8. Put dishes away in the cupboard.

How to Clean the Bathroom

  1. Gather cleaning supplies ‚Äî toilet cleaner, scrubbing brush, disinfectant, gloves, sponge, and mop.
  2. Remove towels, rugs, and personal items from the bathroom.
  3. Spray disinfectant or bathroom cleaner on sinks, counters, and the bathtub or shower area.
  4. Let the cleaner sit for a few minutes to break down soap scum and grime.
  5. Scrub the sink, tub, and shower walls with a sponge or brush.
  6. Apply toilet cleaner inside the bowl and scrub with a toilet brush.
  7. Wipe mirrors and glass surfaces using glass cleaner and a microfiber cloth.
  8. Clean the floor ‚Äî sweep first, then mop using disinfectant solution.

How to Organize the Closet

  1. Empty the entire closet ‚Äî take out clothes, shoes, and accessories.
  2. Sort items into categories: keep, donate, recycle, or discard.
  3. Wipe down shelves, rods, and drawers to remove dust.
  4. Group similar items together (shirts, pants, dresses, jackets, etc.).
  5. Hang clothes neatly using matching hangers ‚Äî organize by type or color.
  6. Fold and stack items like sweaters, jeans, or t-shirts on shelves.
  7. Use boxes or bins for accessories such as belts, scarves, or bags.
  8. Place shoes on a shoe rack or in clear boxes at the bottom of the closet.

How to Feed the Pets

  1. Wash your hands before handling pet food.
  2. Gather the necessary items ‚Äî food, clean bowls, water, and any supplements or treats.
  3. Check your pet‚Äôs feeding schedule and portion size.
  4. Pour the correct amount of food into each pet‚Äôs bowl (use measuring cups if needed).
  5. Provide fresh, clean water ‚Äî replace old water if it‚Äôs been sitting for a while.
  6. If you have multiple pets, ensure each one eats their own food to avoid fights.
  7. After they finish eating, wash the bowls and dry them for next use.
  8. Give your pet a little affection or playtime after meals!
